Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 12, 2025

We Don't Collect Your Personal Data

The simple truth: Godly App doesn't collect names, emails, location data, or any personal information. Your Bible verse plans stay on your phone.

What Data Leaves Your Device?

Only when you request an explanation, your selected Bible verse is sent to OpenAI's API to generate explanations. That's it.

  • OpenAI: Processes verse data per their privacy policy
  • Bible API: Provides public domain Scripture content
  • Apple: Handles subscriptions and billing

What Stays on Your Phone

  • Your verse plans
  • Your custom notes
  • Your reading history
  • Your preferences

We cannot access any of this. It's yours alone.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, GDPR and similar privacy laws don't require complex compliance from us. However:

  • You can delete your data by deleting the app
  • You can request info about OpenAI's data practices at openai.com/privacy
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Children

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child is using the App inappropriately, please contact us.
